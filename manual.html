DE LA MODULAR — Web Style Manual

1. Brand Foundation

Tone: Severe. Direct. Underground. No brand shouting. No marketing fluff. Identity through structure, not decoration.

The brand does not introduce itself loudly. It exists. It is implied.

2. Color Doctrine

Primary Background

#0a0312

Text (Worn Xerox White)

#f0e6fb

Accent (Voltage Pink)

#e84b76

Panels share the same color as the background. Separation happens via lines, not surfaces.

Pink is never decorative. It is a signal.

3. Typography System

Primary Font

"DIN1451", "Bahnschrift", "Arial Narrow", sans-serif

Usage: – Navigation – Body text – Section headers – Structural UI

Experimental Font (Hero Only)

"Bevan", serif

Usage: – Hero statement only – Never body – Never navigation

Hero does not display brand name. Only statement.

4. Logo System

Primary Logo: logo_text.svg Size: 83px × 83px Placement: Top-left, fixed navigation.

Under the logo: 1px micro accent line. Length: ~28px. Purpose: subtle voltage trace.

Pig Logo (logo_pig.svg): – Used as ghosted background in hero – Opacity under 0.05 – No fill distortion – No animation

5. Layout System

Max width: 1200px 12-column grid 40px grid gap 140px vertical section spacing

.grid { display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(12, 1fr); gap: 40px; }

No card shadows. No elevated surfaces. Structure is defined by alignment and thin lines.

6. Motion Policy

Motion must be controlled. Minimal. Intentional.

Allowed:

– Hero jitter (under 1px displacement) – Voltage line expansion – Micro hover transitions

Forbidden:

– Parallax – Scroll hijacking – Continuous pulse – Glow effects – Bounce animations – Large easing curves

Motion introduces tension. Never entertainment.

7. Interaction Rules

Buttons: – Transparent – 1px border – Accent on hover

Panels: – Border only – Accent border on hover

Navigation: – Uppercase – Light letter spacing – Accent on hover

8. What Not To Do

No gradients. No glass morphism. No drop shadows. No neon glow. No startup aesthetics. No unnecessary animation. No decorative color fields.

Serious. Structural. Deliberate.
